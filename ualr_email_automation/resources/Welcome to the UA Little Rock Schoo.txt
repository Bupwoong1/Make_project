Welcome to the UA Little Rock School of Business, where we provide solid, affordable foundations for your future success. With flexible learning options, real-world experiences, and a commitment to excellence, we equip our students to thrive in today’s dynamic business environment.

Many of our undergraduate degrees are offered both in-person and online, giving you the flexibility to shape your education around your needs. Explore our degree programs, minors, and certificate options.

Accreditation and Recognition

AACSB Accredited: The School of Business is proud to be AACSB accredited, a recognition held by less than 5% of business schools worldwide.
2023 US News Ranking: Ranked #32 for our Online Business Program in 2023, our programs stand out for their quality and affordability.
Affordable Education: With tuition starting at just $3,500 per semester and annual scholarships totaling over $200,000, we’re committed to making quality education accessible.
Real-World Experience and Networking
Our students gain practical experience through internships and partnerships with leading companies, preparing them for career success. Through the Arkansas Small Business and Technology Development Center (ASBTDC) and other industry connections, students can gain hands-on experience to set them apart in the job market.

Degree Programs
Accounting
Unleash the power of accounting! This degree not only prepares you for various career paths but also fulfills all the requirements to sit for professional exams like the CPA. Learn from expert faculty, all of whom are CPAs, and take advantage of our strong industry connections and internships with top companies. Join student organizations such as Beta Alpha Psi and the Accounting Society to engage with peers and professionals. Curriculum: Accounting, B.B.A.

Business Analytics
Prepare for a future in the high-growth field of data analytics. Our project-based learning approach and industry-driven curriculum will equip you with practical skills in data analytics, all while offering the flexibility to complete the program entirely online. Employment prospects for data analytics professionals are expected to grow by 35% through 2032. Curriculum, Business Analytics, B.B.A.

Business Information Systems
Develop expertise in technology management and application in organizations. Gain mastery in database systems, programming, systems design, and more. This program offers skills in high demand across industries, from software development to information security analysis. Curriculum: Business Information Systems, B.B.A.

Economics
Think logically and analytically with a degree in Economics. Hone your skills in statistical analysis and critical thinking while gaining hands-on research experience through policy-relevant projects. Graduates are well-prepared for careers in finance, government, and public policy or further studies, including law school. Curriculum: Economics, B.B.A.

Finance
Invest in your future with a Finance degree. Choose from specialized areas like General Finance, Real Estate Finance, and Financial Services. Through experiential learning opportunities like competitions and real-world equity pitch projects, you’ll be prepared to excel in a variety of financial fields. The program also prepares you for CFA Level I.
Curriculum: Finance, General Finance Emphasis, B.B.A.
Curriculum: Finance, Financial Services and Risk Management Emphasis, B.B.A.
Curriculum: Finance, Real Estate Emphasis, B.B.A.

International Business
Study the complexities of the global marketplace with our interdisciplinary International Business program. Develop a comprehensive understanding of international business practices and strategic options that can be applied in a variety of industries. Curriculum: International Business, B.B.A.

Management
Become a future leader with our Management degree. Specialize in areas like Innovation and Entrepreneurship, General Management, or Human Resource Management. Gain real-world insights through internships and collaborative projects. Network with corporate executives to glean career advice and fast-track your success. Curriculum: Management, B.B.A.

Marketing
Master the art of marketing with a focus on Digital Marketing or Professional Sales. Discover how AI-powered marketing can amplify the impact of campaigns, and gain real-world experience through case studies and practical projects. Graduates will be ready to lead in a variety of roles, from product management to digital marketing strategy. Curriculum: Marketing, B.B.A.

Scholarships and Financial Aid
The School of Business offers generous scholarship opportunities for current and incoming students. Scholarships are available for general business students, as well as those in Accounting, Economics, Finance, Management, Marketing, and Technology programs. Applications open in November for awards in the summer, fall, and spring terms.

Career Services
Through our Career Catalyst Program, students can become “career ready” individuals. The program includes on-campus and virtual networking events, career fairs, and internship opportunities. Students also receive professional development training in resume writing, personal branding, interview skills, and more.

Networking: Business Etiquette Luncheons, Career Fairs, Internship Fairs.
Professional Development: Resume writing, salary negotiation workshops, and interview skills.
